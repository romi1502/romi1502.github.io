About me

I am currently a Music Information Retrieval engineer at Deezer. Before Deezer, I worked for 3 years at Audionamix. I did my PhD with TSI (Signal and Image Processing departement) of TELECOM ParisTechunder the supervision of Bertrand David and Roland Badeau.

Research

My work is mainly focused on Music Information Retrieval and my thesis topic dealt with decomposition of musical spectrograms using signal models in factorization methods (such as Non-negative Matrix Factorization).

My work at Audionamix was more focused on audio source separation.
