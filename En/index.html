About me

I am currently a research engineer at Audionamix. I did my PhD with TSI (Signal and Image Processing departement) of TELECOM ParisTech. My phd was supervised by Bertrand David and Roland Badeau.

Research

My work is mainly focused on Music Information Retrieval and my thesis topic dealt with decomposition of musical spectrograms using signal models in factorization methods (such as Non-negative Matrix Factorization).

My work at Audionamix is more focused on audio source separation.
